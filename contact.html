Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I invest in Maji Safi Africa projects?

You can invest through our investment portal on the Invest page. We offer various investment opportunities with different risk profiles and returns. Minimum investment starts at $500. Contact our Investment Director Jester Paul for personalized guidance.

How do I become a water entrepreneur?

We train and support local entrepreneurs to manage our Smart Water ATMs. Contact our Community Director Irene Mgimba to learn about upcoming training programs and opportunities in your region.

What is your response time for inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Investment-related queries are prioritized and typically receive responses within 4 hours.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes, we welcome interns in various departments including technology, community development, and impact assessment. Send your CV and motivation letter to careers@majisafiafrica.co.ke.
