Our Story & Vision

Maji Safi Africa was founded in 2022 with a mission to provide clean water and health services to 100 million Africans through sustainable business models.

Our Guiding Principles

We believe in creating value that benefits communities, investors, and Africa's future

Our Mission

To provide sustainable clean water and health solutions to 100 million Africans by 2035 through innovative technology and community partnerships.

Our Vision

An Africa where every person has access to clean water within 500 meters, and water-borne diseases are eliminated through preventive healthcare.

Our Values

Sustainability, Community Ownership, Transparency, Innovation, and Impact Measurement guide every decision we make.
